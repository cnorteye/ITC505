Addendum

Thank you for visiting my resume page. Because this is a professional site, I wanted to keep the styling choices simple and focus on providing the most important information in an easily readable format. Below is a breakdown of the reasoning behind my styling choices.

Font Choices - Family and Size

I used the Arial, sans-serif font family because it is widely used for websites. For font size, my main focus was consistency throughout the site and ensuring readability. For headings, I used 2em, which is a larger font size, to provide a clear hierarchy and make it easier for visitors to understand the flow of information. The body text is set to the standard 16px to ensure readability across different devices and screen resolutions.

Color Choices

I used a darker green background color with contrasting mustard yellow text to ensure readability and provide a modern, professional look. The contrast ensures that important information stands out and is visible to visitors. Additionally, the main background is a light gray, which also helps with readability.

Margins and Padding

The overall layout is simple, with a main header, a sidebar using the aside tag, and a main section contained within a div. I separated the work experience section from the qualifications section to make the information easily accessible without excessive scrolling. I used padding: 20px in the header to ensure the text does not touch the edges, providing comfortable spacing. Margins were added to ensure enough spacing between elements, improving the visual structure of the page.
